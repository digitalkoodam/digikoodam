About Digital Koodam

We are a diverse and dynamic group having a common goal of taking the ancient Koodam concept to everyone via modern media. We believe in open sharing of knowledge, resources and efforts for the common good. Our aim is for all of us members of the society to come together, share knowledge, build spaces for dialogue, explore our differences, celebrate our similarities, rejuvenate the human connection and revel in the solidarity of one universal family.

Vision

DigitalKoodam envisions a just society where universal dignity and fraternity are valued and sought for actively by every member. For this, we need spaces where one has an equal voice, differences are explored amicably and community ties are strengthened.

Mission

Towards the realization of an open, just society, we make it our mission to create a platform where people from every strata of society can join together and learn, discuss and share. We model ourselves on the ancient concept of KOODAM used by our ancestors.
